How to Play

Single Player Mode

  • Match all pairs before running out of moves to advance to the next level
  • Each level increases the number of cards and gives you more moves
  • Complete matches quickly to earn bonus points

Two Player Mode

  • Take turns with another player to find matching pairs
  • When you find a match, you get another turn
  • The player with the most matches at the end wins

Scoring

  • Single Player: Score based on level reached and moves remaining
  • Two Player: Each match is worth 1 point
